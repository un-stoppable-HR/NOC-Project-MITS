Dear Students,
All the students, whoever is going for any Internship / Project / Training in any Organization, are required to take NO OBJECTION CERTFICATE (NOC) for the same.

Do follow the procedure of applying online:-

  1. Take care of information you are providing in the online form which will be printed as it is in the NOC for Internship/Training.
  2. Very Important: Do confirm and write carefully the Name, Designation and Company Name & Address in the form to whom you are sending letter/NOC in that company/organization. It will be printed as it is. If you do mistake in writing spelling of any name, designation or company name, it will not be accepted any where. Even a minor mistake of Comma (,) will not be considered.
  3. You will receive NOC via Email through T&P Cell. It will be an automatically generated letter/NOC with date and reference number after approval of your Faculty Coordinator/Internship Coordinator of your Department and then from T&P Cell. For further proofs you can directly take the print of email with all details and live link of NOC at our drive if any further proof is required by any organization.

IMPORTANT NOTE: Do Read each and every instruction given in the Online Form and below each field

For any query: You may contact to your Faculty Coordinator.
